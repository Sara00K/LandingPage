Hologram Shader

Hi there! I am excited to show you a hologram shader I created last month with some additional tweaks. Let's starts with the basics: Getting Coordinates //PASAR ESTE COMO HEADES Y SECCION I first created this shader with the porpouse of using it for a 2D map applied on a simple plane, and later on added the possibility of applying it on any 3D model. For these two poruposes I had to get coordinates depending on the use: UV Coordinates //Mini Seccion For the map I got the coordinates simply from the object UVs, as I didn't need anything complex, and from this I mask the direction needed for the lines direction (Mask on the G Channel for Unreal). AGREGAR FOTO DE LOS UVS y el degradado. World Coordinates //Mini Seccion To apply to any kind of 3D model, and ensure that the flow of the lines would be consistent, I got the coordinates from the Absolute World Position //Podria poner foto de esa parte del shader With the coordinates on place, the next steps flowed nicely, let's check out what I used the coordinates for: //SECCION -> Color Gradient After getting the coordinates being on the UV or the World Position I applied a lerp to create a color gradient between two values, the base color and the top color, with additional parameters to tweak the transition softness and where it would start. //SECCION -> lines As an important part of the hologram I got the lines, the base from these is the fract math operation.
